Who We Are

Fighting hunger
one campus
at a time

NourishU is a not-for-profit dedicated to ending malnutrition among university students in Sydney. We know that the cost of living crisis, academic stress, and lack of nutritional knowledge combine to leave far too many students struggling just to eat.

We believe that no student should face their future on an empty stomach. So we show up — with free meals, on-campus pantries, nutrition education, and a network of Sydney food businesses who care just as much as we do.

The Research

The numbers don't lie

Food insecurity among Australian university students is not a fringe issue — it is a growing crisis, backed by research. Here is what the data tells us.

53%

of university students experienced food insecurity in 2024 — up from 42% in 2022. That's an 11 percentage point increase in just two years.

Australian Student Food Security Study, 2024
1 in 4

students were experiencing severe food insecurity, with diet quality declining sharply as insecurity increased.

Australian Student Food Security Study, 2024
70%

of food-insecure students reported mild to extremely severe depressive symptoms. Food insecurity was identified as a significant predictor of higher depression scores.

Australian Student Wellbeing Report, 2023
↑

Financial worry triggers psychological distress, which causes physiological responses. When stressed, students crave and consume higher amounts of high-calorie food — worsening nutritional outcomes.

Stress & Eating Behaviour Research, 2023
🏫

Universities often run food pantries to provide students with basic supplies — but researchers agree they also need more long-term structural supports for students. Short-term relief is not enough. NourishU is building that longer-term system.

University Food Security Framework Review, 2024
Programs

What we do

Everything we offer is free, stigma-free, and designed around students' real lives — not bureaucratic processes.

⭐ Flagship
🍽️

Free Hot Meal Program

Our flagship program delivers freshly prepared, nutritious hot meals to students on campus several times a week. Partnering with local Sydney restaurants and caterers, every meal is balanced, culturally diverse, and absolutely free. No ID checks. No income verification. Just food.

🛒

Campus Pantry

Stocked pantry hubs across our Sydney partner campuses — open before, between, and after classes.

🥗

Nutrition Workshops

Hands-on workshops with nutritionists and peer mentors to help you eat well on a student budget.

💼

Job Connect

Our Sydney business partners advertise part-time roles exclusively for NourishU students, with hours built around study.

📦

Emergency Food Packs

Immediate support for students in crisis — a 3-day food pack delivered discreetly to your campus, same week.
